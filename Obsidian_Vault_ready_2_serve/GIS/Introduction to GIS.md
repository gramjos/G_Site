_Definition_ **Geographic Information System (GIS)** 
A GIS is a computer-based system to aid in the collection, maintenance, storage, analysis, output and distribution of spatial data and information. 
### Applied Geography 
- Applied geography in the form maps and spatial information is a GIS. 
- Key to all GIs definitions are the 'what' and 'where' 
###### Common GIS workflow
1. Data entry
	- Capture coordinates, attribute data, data import
2. Editting
	- point/line/polygon creation, error detection. 
3. Data management
	- versioning, compression, documentation, projection
 4. Analysis
	 - spatial overlay, buffering, attribute query, terrain analysis, etc..
5. Output 
	- map design and layout, digital graphic production, export format generation
